About Hoshi

The calmness of Japan, and the rich, generous hospitality of Hyderabad.

The result is a cafe that's both meticulous and warm — clean lines, quiet music and food that respects local flavors.

HOSHI (星) means "star" in Japanese. We are a speciality coffee shop in the heart of Madhapur, built for those who believe a great cup of coffee deserves unhurried attention.

The Philosophy

静けさ。
Stillness.

shizukesa — the quality of calm

In a city that never stops, HOSHI offers a reason to pause. The coffee counter sits at the centre — watch each pour, each bloom, each extraction. Stay as long as you like.
